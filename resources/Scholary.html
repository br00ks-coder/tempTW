John Doe's Web Garden

Introduction

The website purpose is to allow users to buy and monitor plants and also get notification based on events that trigger messages delivered to their email address.

Structure

Any user is welcomed to the main page where he can login/register. Besides that he also can access the about us page that has information regarding the website, and the contact page for questions if there are any

After the login process is done, the user is presented with his/her/etc profile where he can edit his information. Also in the menu bar two webpages are unlocked for monitor and buying plants.
